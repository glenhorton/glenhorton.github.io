About Glen

I'm Glen. I spent my childhood in Chicago, but I've spent most of my life in SouthWest Ohio. I currently live in Cincinnati with my family and two hungry cats.

Over the past few years I've made lots of big changes to live a more meaningful and less stressful life. So I now consider myself a recovering perfectionist. I've also embraced minimalism so I can focus on the things that matter in my life and simplify as much as possible (like this simple website). That means owning less, doing less, and worrying less. It's a work in progress.

I'm cultivating several areas of interest right now:

  • Exploring and practicing Secular Buddhism
  • Practicing yoga and meditation
  • Neurohacking
  • Exercising
  • Spending time in nature
  • Learning to juggle (it's hard)
  • Exploring new music
  • Reading

I'm passionate about libraries and free access to information. I'm also passionate about technology, especially open source software and web applications. I started supporting technologies in libraries in 1992 while I attended Wright State University. I've worked with all types of libraries over the years and I'm now a senior software developer for the University of Cincinnati Libraries. Most of my time is spent developing open source projects like Scholar@UC.
