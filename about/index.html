About Glen

I'm Glen. I live with my wife and two kids in Cincinnati where we belong to two cats. I'm from Chicago, but moved to SouthWest Ohio with my family when I was a kid. My wife and I met ninth grade (but she wasn't my wife back then).

I started supporting technologies in libraries in 1992 while I attended Wright State University. I've worked with all types of libraries over the years, but I'm now a software developer for the University of Cincinnati Libraries working on open source projects like Scholar@UC.

I'm recovering perfectionist. I was never perfect, but the struggle to try and be perfect eventually led to a lot of stress. So I'm not trying so hard anymore. I've been practicing minimalism to focus on the things that are important in life and rid myself of the rest. Meditation and yoga help with that too!
