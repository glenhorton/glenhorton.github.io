About Glen

@ Work

I am a software developer for the University of Cincinnati Libraries. We are currently building a next-generation digital repository based on the open source Hydra Project.

I previously served as the Digital Services Manager for Campbell County Public Library where I was responsible for all things digital including the library's digital branch, online events, and social media. Before that, I spent eleven years as the Technology Coordinator & Assistant Director for SWON Libraries where I provided technology consultation and technology training for libraries.

My areas of expertise include web development, social media, open source software, mobile computing, and speaking about technology.

@ Home

I live in Cincinnati with my wife, two kids, and two hungry cats. I am very much a geek, but am often mis-labeled a dork. I try to spend my free time enjoying music and exploring new technology, but I usually just get suckered into playing with my kids.
